About Us

Kotasara Piyangala Rajamaha Viharaya at Bibile is an ancient temple dating back to King Devanampiyatissa (250-210 BC) of the Anuradhapura Kingdom,
according to ancient Chronicles.
Thupawansa, the Chronicle of the Great Stupa, recounts a story about this temple.
During the construction of the Great Ruwanweliseya Stupa by King Dutugemunu (161-131 BC), a priest from Kotasara Piyangala Viharaya wished to donate a brick. However,
the king had instructed that only materials purchased by him be used. The priest befriended a brick builder, measured a brick precisely, made one himself, and added it to the pile.
When King Dutugemunu discovered this,
he questioned the builder, who revealed that a priest from Kotasara Piyangala Viharaya had given him the brick.
